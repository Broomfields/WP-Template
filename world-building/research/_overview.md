# Research

The wiki is the world's source of truth, but it doesn't write itself. This folder is where I do the work that makes the wiki believable — studying real-world history, systems, concepts, and phenomena so that what ends up in the wiki holds together under scrutiny.

That might mean researching an actual historical period because my setting draws from it. It might mean looking into how a particular social system works in the real world before building a fictional version of it. It might mean understanding the geography or geology behind a landscape I want to create. Whatever it is, if I'm studying something in order to make the world more coherent, it lives here.

## How It's Organised

Research is grouped by topic. Each topic gets its own folder, named clearly after the subject. Inside a topic folder, break the research down into numbered files covering different angles — key figures, key concepts, historical timeline, social effects, whatever makes sense for that particular subject.

There's no rigid schema for what goes inside a topic folder because every subject demands something different. Just organise it in whatever way makes the subject navigable.
