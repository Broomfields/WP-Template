# Environments

The biomes, terrain features, climate zones, and ecosystems of [World Name] — the physical backdrop that shapes where peoples settle, what they eat, how they fight, and what they're afraid of. I use these to attach consistent ecological context to locations, flora, fauna, and cultures without duplicating the same notes in every entry.

Worth noting: Environments and Locations work together but aren't the same thing. An environment is a *type* of place — a swamp, an arctic plain, a volcanic field. A location is a *specific named* place — the Ashfen Marshes, the Northern Wastes, Ember Peak. Environments exist so I can attach consistent ecological context across flora, fauna, and multiple locations without duplicating those notes in every individual location entry.
