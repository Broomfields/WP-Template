# Cosmology

The deep structure of reality in [World Name] — deities, planes of existence, cosmic forces, creation myths, and the laws that underpin everything else.

Worth noting: Cosmology and Magic are related but deliberately separate. Cosmology is the "why reality is structured this way" — the metaphysical framework, the deities, the planes. Magic is the "how people interact with that structure" — the systems, techniques, and disciplines. They'll reference each other constantly, but keeping them separate stops the fundamental architecture of the world getting buried under practical system details. A deity lives here; a cultivation technique lives in Magic.
