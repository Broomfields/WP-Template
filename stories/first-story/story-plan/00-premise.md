# Premise

## The Core Premise

_In one or two sentences: what's this story actually about, and why is it worth telling?_

## The Central Conflict

_What's the primary tension driving things forward? What's pushing against what?_

## The Central Question

_What question does the story pose that the reader needs answered? This is plot-level — distinct from the themes._

## The Promise

_What does this story promise the reader in terms of experience, tone, and emotional journey? What are they signing up for?_

## Setting & Context

_Brief orienting context: when, where, and in what state is the world when this story begins?_
